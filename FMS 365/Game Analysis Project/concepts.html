Concepts & Theory

Interactivity

Interactivity refers to the ways players engage with a game system. In True Colors, interactivity is built through dialogue choices, exploration, and emotional interpretation rather than action.

Agency

Agency is the player’s ability to influence the game. While the story has a fixed structure, players shape relationships and emotional outcomes, creating a strong sense of personal involvement.

Player vs Character

The game creates tension between player control and Alex’s identity. Players guide her decisions, but she still has a defined personality, limiting total control.

Immersion

Immersion is achieved through atmosphere, music, and emotional storytelling. The game encourages players to slow down and connect with the world.
